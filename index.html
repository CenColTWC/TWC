Description

“Together We Can” is here for the people of Toronto (GTA). Our purpose is to provide emergency or general services to the residents of Toronto in order to: report the important issues or suggestions which need attention & consideration. Every responsible citizen of Toronto expects and hopes for Toronto, to be a better place to live. Through this website we provide opportunity for people to report any incidence, complaint or suggest something anonymously or by becoming a registered user of Website. Some of the services from the Website are listed below: Report situations where a person or property is in immediate danger but requires attention of the Toronto Police. Report or suggest basic services to Toronto Transportation Company in order to improve the services for the passengers. Report situations or suggest ideas to the Toronto Municipal Corporation to serve the city better and make its environment more sustainable and clean. Suggest basic services or ideas that residents of Toronto expect or want to suggest. We are here to know you more and to serve you for better. Hence, together we can take a step forward to make our GTA a better place to live. Suggest or Report us as you are a priority and your ideas will make our services better.
